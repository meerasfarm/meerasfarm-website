Our Story

A Farm Built From
The Whole World

Every corner of Meera's Farm tells a story. The exposed bricks were fired in Godhra, Gujarat. The teak furniture was handcrafted by artisans in Saharanpur. The roof tiles came from Monier in Nashik.

And then the journey goes further — Balinese statues, Central American folk art, Himalayan pieces from Nepal. This is not a farm stay. It is a curated world, assembled with love, waiting for you to walk through it.

A sacred space blessed by Lord Ganesha — free from alcohol, devoted to peace and genuine human connection.
